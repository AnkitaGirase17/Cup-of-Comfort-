Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cup of Comfort! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website to browse or order tea and coffee online.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to ensure a seamless ordering and delivery experience. The information we collect includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment details.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, order history, and browser/device type.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and deliver your tea or coffee orders.
  • Send order updates, confirmations, and promotional offers.
  • Improve our website experience and customer service.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Sensitive data such as payment details are handled through secure, encrypted third-party payment processors.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (e.g., payment gateways, analytics, delivery partners) that collect limited information necessary to perform their services. These providers have their own privacy policies and are required to protect your data.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and offer personalized content. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date. Continued use of our website after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
